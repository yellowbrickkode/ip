### **Demeter**

> "When you go to bed with a clear head, you will not get up with a headache."

Demeter keeps your brain tidy, your mind clear.

It’s a _text-based, fast and simple_ solution to the mess in your head. 

**All you have to do is:**
1. Download from [here](https://github.com/yellowbrickkode/ip/releases). 
2. Run.
3. Add tasks.
4. Relax. 😎

That’s it. Easy as that. 

**Features include:** 
- Managing tasks and deadlines 
- Auto-save (because forgetting is the definition of being human)

Your mind could soon look like: 
- [ ] homework 
- [ ] rest and relax 
- [ ] meet up with your friends

        new Demeter("data/demeter.txt").run();
    }`
